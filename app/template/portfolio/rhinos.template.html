Jax Zoo Rhino Exhibit

Skills used:

Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver

Rhino species are disappearing at an alarming rate. Poaching has increased in the last year and not enough people know about how heavily rhinos affect their surrounding environment. Most people are also not aware of the Jacksonville Chapter of Bowling for Rhinos, an AAZK organization dedicated to protecting rhinos.

The goal of our redesign for the rhino overlook was to raise awareness for the endangered status of rhinos as well as inform our audience about rhinos as an umbrella species. We also implemented several Bowling for Rhinos elements within the exhibit, in hopes to generate: traffic to the event, the website, and lastly, larger donations. To spark the most interest, from the largest distribution of demographics possible, we incorporated a level of interactivity within each element designed for the exhibit.
